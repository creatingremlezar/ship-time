üïê Ship Time vs Local Time Explained

Ship Time: Royal Caribbean keeps the ship on one consistent time zone throughout the cruise (usually the departure port's time zone). This means all onboard activities, dining, and announcements follow "Ship Time."

Local Time: Each port has its own local time zone. When you go ashore, you'll need to be aware of the local time for meeting times, tour schedules, and getting back to the ship.

Important: All ship departure times are announced in Ship Time, so always convert back when planning your port day!
